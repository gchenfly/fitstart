Frequently asked questions

Answers to the usual suspects.

Is FitStart good for beginners?

Yes. FitStart is designed for people who want simple, approachable workouts with step-by-step guidance and short sessions.

Do I need equipment?

Most starter workouts are designed to be easy to begin at home. Add equipment only if future workout plans include it.

Can I track my progress?

Yes. The app includes progress views for calories, workout minutes, completed sessions, and achievements.

What if I miss a workout?

No problem. Resume anytime. Fitness progress is built on consistency, not perfection.

How do I report a bug?

Email support with the issue details, your device model, operating system version, and screenshots if available.

Can I suggest new features?

Absolutely. Feedback helps improve future workout types, app features, and the overall experience.
